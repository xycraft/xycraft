About me

I am a PhD student at the Human-Centered Computing & Intelligent Sensing Lab (HiLab), Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE), University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), advised by Professor Yang Zhang.

My research sits at Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and sensing, focusing on how energy and information flow during physical human-object interactions can enhance the context-awareness, connectivity and ubiquity of computing systems. My work often involves a combination of hardware fabrication and software engineering, integrating electronics, computer vision and machine learning to create innovative solutions. I believe that technology should be designed to simplify everyday life, while also enriching real-life experiences through enjoyable and meaningful interactions.

Feel free to email me if you are interested in my work, or if you would like to collaborate on a project! I am always open to new ideas and opportunities.
